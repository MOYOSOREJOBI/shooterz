How to Play

Objective

Survive endless waves of enemies. Every wave is faster — stay alive as long as you can.

Desktop Controls

  • ↑↓←→ or WASD — Move
  • Mouse pointer on canvas — Aim & auto-fire simultaneously. No clicking needed.

Mobile Controls

  • Left side — Virtual joystick (move)
  • Right side — Touch to aim & fire
  • Landscape mode required

Enemies

  • ● Chasers — Fast red circles, 30 HP, 2 shots to kill
  • ● Tanks — Slow purple circles, 90 HP, 5 shots to kill

Multiplayer Modes

  • Solo — Classic offline / online survival
  • Co-op — 2-4 players vs waves together
  • 1v1 / 1v1v1 / 1v1v1v1 — Free-for-all + waves. 500 player-bullets per kill.
  • 2v2 — Team vs team + waves. Last team standing wins.

Tips

  • Pick up green drops for +30 HP
  • Waves spawn every 2 s after clearing — keep moving
