How to Play

Objective

Survive endless waves of enemies. The balls come faster each wave — stay alive as long as you can!

Desktop Controls

  • ↑ ↓ ← → or W A S D — Move
  • Mouse — Aim & shoot simultaneously (just move the mouse = auto-fire)

Mobile Controls

  • Left side — Virtual joystick to move
  • Right side — Tap & drag to aim and shoot
  • Rotate phone to landscape for best experience

Enemies

  • Chasers — Fast, 30 HP. 2 shots to kill.
  • Tanks — Slow but tough, 90 HP. 5 shots to kill.

Game Modes

  • Solo — Survive alone. Classic mode.
  • Co-op — 2-4 players on the same team vs waves.
  • 1v1 / 1v1v1 / 1v1v1v1 — Free-for-all. Kill players with 500 bullets.
  • 2v2 — Team deathmatch + waves. Last team standing wins.

Tips

  • Pick up green orbs dropped by enemies to heal 30 HP.
  • Waves arrive every 2 seconds after all enemies are cleared.
  • Player bullets deal 0.2 dmg (500 bullets to eliminate a player).
